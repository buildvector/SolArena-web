# SolArena Season 1 – The 7 Day War

Season 1 is the first competitive launch phase of SolArena.

The purpose of Season 1:
- Stress-test the PvP system under real usage
- Activate burn multiplier mechanics
- Create visible on-chain competition
- Establish early leaderboard legends

Duration:
- 7 days
- Starts at token launch
- Ends automatically at fixed timestamp

Core objectives:
- Compete in PvP games
- Climb leaderboard rankings
- Burn tokens for multiplier boosts
- Generate volume through skill-based play

Leaderboard Categories:
- Total PvP Points
- Fee Contribution Points
- Win Streak Bonus
- ELO Skill Rating
- Burn Multiplier Score

No promised rewards:
- No guaranteed airdrops
- No financial promises
- No ROI expectations

However:
- Historical leaderboard data is permanently stored
- Top players may receive recognition
- Future seasons may reference Season 1 results

Season 1 Philosophy:
- Fair launch energy
- Transparent mechanics
- Skill > Capital
- Sustainable hype over artificial pump

End of Season:
- Leaderboard snapshot
- Burn statistics snapshot
- Public recap post
- Transition into Season 2 roadmap

Season 1 is designed as:
A proving ground.
Not a promise machine.
