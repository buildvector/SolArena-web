# SolArena – Season 1 Scope

## Core Games
Season 1 includes three playable games:

- Flip (RNG based)
- Reaction Duel (skill based)
- Tic Tac Toe (PvP strategy)

All games are integrated into the shared leaderboard system.

---

## Leaderboard Model

- Points based on volume and wins
- Burn multiplier applied to scoring
- Tier-based boost system
- Anti-manipulation checks
- Weekly ranking reset (optional for later phase)

---

## Burn Multiplier Model

- Tier-based scaling
- Diminishing returns structure
- Hard capped at Tier 10
- Multiplier range: 1.0 – 1.6
- Designed to reward participation without allowing domination

---

## Token Utility

- Boost leaderboard score
- Increase visibility in rankings
- Potential Season-based rewards (non-promised)
- Governance potential (future)

---

## Launch Structure

Soft Launch:
- Open platform
- Monitor stability
- Observe organic usage

Hard Launch:
- Token activation
- Burn leaderboard war
- Public Season announcement

---

## Season 1 Objective

- Test PvP engagement
- Validate burn multiplier mechanics
- Establish early competitive community
- Collect real usage data
